Your Journey to the Holy Land

The Majlis facilitates the sacred pilgrimage to Mecca and Medina for Ethiopian Muslims, ensuring a spiritually fulfilling and well-organized journey.

As the official body coordinating Hajj for Ethiopian pilgrims, we work closely with the Saudi Arabian authorities to ensure smooth visa processing, comfortable accommodation, and comprehensive support throughout your pilgrimage.

Our experienced team provides guidance from registration to return, including pre-departure training, on-ground support in Saudi Arabia, and spiritual guidance throughout the journey.

Required Documents

Prepare the following for your Hajj/Umrah application:

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Completed application form
  • Medical certificate
  • Vaccination records (Meningitis)
  • Proof of payment

Important Dates

Key dates for Hajj 2025:

  • Registration Opens: January 2025
  • Registration Deadline: March 2025
  • Pre-departure Training: May 2025
  • Departure: June 2025
  • Hajj Days: Dhul Hijjah 8-12
