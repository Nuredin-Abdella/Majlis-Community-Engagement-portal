Empowering the Future

Building a stronger community through education, skills, and Islamic values

Our Objective

To empower and uplift women and youth in Ethiopia by integrating Islamic values, education, and skills training to foster holistic development and socio‑economic growth.

Our comprehensive program addresses the unique needs of women and youth, providing them with the tools and resources needed to become active, engaged members of their communities while staying true to Islamic principles.

Our Program Components

Integrated approaches to holistic development

Islamic Education & Skill Development

  • Centers for Islamic studies and vocational training
  • Courses in entrepreneurship, technology, and healthcare
  • Ethics and values-based curriculum

Financial Inclusion

  • Financial literacy programs
  • Interest-free financing options

Healthcare & Well-being

  • Health awareness campaigns
  • Islamic perspective on wellness
  • Mobile healthcare services

Leadership Development

  • Leadership training programs
  • Community service initiatives
  • Civic engagement workshops

Technology & Innovation

Monitoring & Evaluation
