Key Initiatives

Making a difference in the lives of women and youth across Ethiopia

Women's Leadership Council

The Women's Leadership Council provides a platform for Muslim women to participate in decision-making processes and community leadership. Through this initiative, we aim to amplify women's voices and ensure their perspectives are represented in all aspects of community life.

The council organizes regular meetings, workshops, and conferences to address issues affecting Muslim women and develop solutions together.

Youth Empowerment Program

Our Youth Empowerment Program focuses on developing the next generation of Muslim leaders through education, mentorship, and practical experience. Young people are given opportunities to lead projects, organize events, and contribute to community development.

The program includes annual youth conferences, leadership camps, and scholarship opportunities for outstanding young Muslims.
