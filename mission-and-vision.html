Mission and Vision

Our Vision

The Majlis’s institutional vision is to see a vibrant Ethiopian Muslim community living Islam in accordance with Allah’s will.

Our Mission

To create a responsible, organized, conscientious, and competent Muslim community with a higher spiritual, social, and economic life that actively contributes to national peace and development, in collaboration with all Ethiopians, by establishing a modern management system based on ethics and skills in the context of Islamic teachings.

The country's highest Islamic authority, representing Ethiopian Muslims both domestically and abroad.
