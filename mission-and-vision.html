Our Mission

The institutional mission of the Majlis is to create a responsible, organized, conscientious, and competent Muslim community with a higher spiritual, social, and economic life that will actively contribute to the peace and development of the nation in collaboration with all Ethiopians by establishing a modern management system based on ethics and skills in the context of Islamic teachings.

Our Vision

The Majlis's institutional vision is to see a vibrant Ethiopian Muslim community living Islam in accordance with Allah's will, contributing positively to national harmony and development through Islamic values and principles.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Faith (Iman)

Grounded in the teachings of the Holy Quran and the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).

Unity (Ummah)

Fostering unity among Muslims and peaceful coexistence with all Ethiopians.

Service (Khidmah)

Dedicated to serving the community with excellence and compassion.

Justice (Adl)

Upholding fairness and justice in all our dealings and decisions.

Integrity (Amanah)

Maintaining honesty and trustworthiness in all our actions.

Knowledge (Ilm)

Promoting Islamic education and continuous learning.

Strategic Objectives

Our key focus areas for serving the Muslim community

Religious Guidance

Provide authentic Islamic guidance and religious services to the Muslim community across Ethiopia.

Education

Promote Islamic education and establish quality educational institutions throughout the country.

Community Development

Support social welfare programs and community development initiatives.

Heritage Preservation

Protect and preserve Islamic heritage sites and cultural treasures.
