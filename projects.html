Our Major Projects

The Ethiopian Islamic Affairs Supreme Council is engaged in several major development projects aimed at improving the lives of Muslim communities across Ethiopia. These projects focus on education, infrastructure, and community development.

In Progress

Mota Islamic Center Project

Mota, Amhara Region 2022 - Present

The Mota Islamic Center is a comprehensive development project that includes a modern mosque, Islamic school, and community center. The project aims to provide quality Islamic education and community services to the Muslim population in the region.

  • Capacity for 5,000 worshippers
  • Modern Islamic school with 24 classrooms
  • Community hall and library
  • Vocational training center
65% Complete

Ongoing

Alfa Development Project

Multiple Locations 2020 - 2025

A comprehensive community development initiative focusing on education, healthcare, and economic empowerment in underserved Muslim communities across Ethiopia. The project has already impacted over 50,000 beneficiaries.

  • Construction of 15 new schools
  • Healthcare outreach programs
  • Vocational training centers
  • Water and sanitation facilities
80% Complete

Completed

African Union Grand Mosque

Addis Ababa, Ethiopia 2018 - 2022

A landmark project featuring one of the largest and most architecturally significant mosques in Africa, located at the African Union headquarters. The mosque serves both the diplomatic community and local residents.

  • Capacity for 15,000 worshippers
  • Modern Islamic architecture
  • Conference facilities
  • Islamic cultural center

Awolia Water & Sanitation

Oromia & Somali Regions 2021 - 2024

An initiative to provide clean water and sanitation facilities to drought-affected communities, improving health outcomes and reducing waterborne diseases in rural Ethiopia.

  • Construction of 50 deep wells
  • Sanitation facilities for schools
  • Hygiene education programs
  • Community water committees
