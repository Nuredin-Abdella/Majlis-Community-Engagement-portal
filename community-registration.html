Register Your Community

Register your Islamic community, mosque, or organization with the Ethiopian Islamic Affairs Supreme Council to gain official recognition and access to our support services.

Registration connects your community to the national network of Islamic institutions and enables you to participate in Majlis programs and initiatives.

  • Official recognition from the national Islamic authority
  • Access to Majlis resources and support services
  • Participation in national Islamic programs
  • Connection to regional and national networks
  • Eligibility for community development grants
  • Training and capacity building opportunities
