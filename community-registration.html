About Community Organization Registration

The Majlis facilitates the registration of Islamic community organizations across Ethiopia, ensuring they operate in accordance with both Islamic principles and Ethiopian laws.

Our Role

EIASC serves as the official body responsible for registering and overseeing Islamic community organizations, ensuring they align with both religious principles and national regulations.

Our Mission

To empower Islamic community organizations by providing them with the necessary recognition, support, and resources to serve their communities effectively.

Our Support

Beyond registration, we offer capacity building, networking opportunities, and guidance to help your organization thrive and make a meaningful impact.

Registration Process

Follow these steps to register your community organization with EIASC

1

Initial Inquiry

Contact our office or visit our website to understand the registration requirements and process.

Timeframe: 1-2 business days

2

Document Preparation

Gather and prepare all required documents as per the checklist provided.

Timeframe: Varies by organization

3

Application Submission

Submit your completed application form along with all required documents to the EIASC office.

Timeframe: 1 business day

4

Review & Verification

Our team will review your application and may conduct site visits or request additional information.

Timeframe: 10-15 business days

5

Approval & Certificate

Upon approval, your organization will be registered and receive an official certificate of registration.

Timeframe: 3-5 business days after approval

Registration Requirements

Documents and information needed for community organization registration

Required Documents

  • Completed Application Form

    Duly filled and signed application form (available at our office or for download)

  • Constitution & Bylaws

    Official document outlining the organization's purpose, structure, and operating procedures

  • Founding Members List

    Complete list of founding members with their full names, IDs, and contact information

  • Board Members Information

    Names, positions, contact details, and CVs of all board/executive committee members

  • Physical Address Proof

    Documentation proving your organization's physical address (lease agreement, utility bill, etc.)

  • Financial Plan

    Detailed budget and funding sources for the organization's activities

  • Activity Plan

    Detailed plan of proposed activities, programs, and projects for the coming year

Additional Notes:

  • All documents must be submitted in Amharic or English
  • Copies of identification documents for all board members
  • Passport-sized photographs of board members
  • Application processing fee receipt (non-refundable)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum of 10 founding members
  • Clear Islamic religious or social objectives
  • Non-profit orientation
  • Compliance with Ethiopian laws
  • No political affiliations
  • Transparent governance structure

Fees

Application Fee: ETB 1,000
Registration Fee: ETB 5,000
Annual Renewal: ETB 2,000

* Fees are subject to change. Contact us for the most current fee structure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about community organization registration

The registration process typically takes 15-30 working days from the date of submitting a complete application. This includes document review, verification, and the approval process. Delays may occur if additional information or clarification is required.

Registered organizations enjoy several benefits including official recognition, access to capacity building programs, networking opportunities, eligibility for certain grants and funding, and the ability to operate legally within the Ethiopian Islamic community framework.

While foreign nationals can participate in organizational activities, the majority of board/executive committee members must be Ethiopian citizens as per national regulations. Specific restrictions may apply to certain leadership positions.

If your application is rejected, you will receive a written explanation for the decision. You may address the issues raised and reapply after 30 days. In some cases, you may appeal the decision following the appeals process outlined in the rejection letter.

Registration must be renewed annually. You will receive a renewal notice 60 days before your current registration expires. Failure to renew on time may result in penalties or revocation of your registration status.

Have more questions? Contact our registration team for assistance.

Terms and Conditions

1. Registration and Approval

Registration with EIASC is subject to approval based on compliance with Islamic principles and Ethiopian laws. EIASC reserves the right to approve or reject any application at its sole discretion.

2. Compliance

Registered organizations must comply with all applicable laws and regulations, maintain accurate records, and submit annual reports as required.

3. Non-Discrimination

Organizations must not discriminate based on ethnicity, gender, or any other protected characteristic in their membership or activities.

4. Financial Transparency

Organizations must maintain transparent financial records and submit to audits as required by EIASC or government authorities.

5. Use of EIASC Name and Logo

Use of the EIASC name, logo, or any official insignia requires prior written authorization.

6. Termination

EIASC reserves the right to suspend or revoke registration if an organization violates these terms or engages in activities contrary to Islamic principles or national laws.

7. Amendments

EIASC may amend these terms and conditions at any time, and it is the responsibility of registered organizations to stay informed of any changes.
