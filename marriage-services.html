Marriage Process

Step-by-step guide to getting married through the Ethiopian Islamic Affairs Supreme Council

1

Initial Inquiry

Contact our marriage services department to inquire about requirements and schedule an appointment.

Timeframe: 1-2 business days

2

Document Submission

Submit all required documents for verification and approval.

Required: See document checklist below

3

Pre-Marital Counseling

Attend mandatory pre-marital counseling sessions with our qualified counselors.

Sessions: 2-3 sessions (1-2 hours each)

4

Marriage Contract

Sign the Islamic marriage contract (Aqd Nikah) in the presence of witnesses and an authorized marriage officiant.

5

Civil Registration

Register the marriage with the appropriate civil authorities to ensure legal recognition.

Note: Required for all civil purposes

Marriage Requirements

Documents and information needed for Islamic marriage registration

For Both Bride and Groom

  • Valid ID

    Original and copy of national ID, passport, or refugee ID

  • Birth Certificate

    Original and copy of birth certificate or age verification document

  • Marital Status Affidavit

    Notarized affidavit confirming single status (for never married) or divorce/death certificate (if previously married)

  • Passport Photos

    Two recent passport-sized photographs with white background

Additional Requirements

  • Witnesses

    Two Muslim male witnesses (or one male and two females) with valid IDs

  • Wali (Guardian)

    Presence of the bride's Wali (father or appointed guardian) with valid ID

  • Mahr (Dower)

    Agreed upon Mahr amount and terms (to be specified in the contract)

Important Notes:

  • All documents must be in Amharic, Arabic, or English
  • Foreign documents must be legalized and translated if not in the required languages
  • Additional documents may be required in special cases
  • Fees apply for the marriage certificate and related services

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Islamic marriage services

According to Ethiopian law, the legal age for marriage is 18 years for both males and females. The Ethiopian Islamic Affairs Supreme Council adheres to both Islamic principles and national laws regarding marriage age. In exceptional cases where one party is between 16-18 years old, special permission may be granted by the court with parental consent, but this is generally discouraged unless there are compelling circumstances.

According to Islamic law, a Muslim woman is not permitted to marry a non-Muslim man unless he converts to Islam. This is based on Quranic teachings and the consensus of Islamic scholars. The primary reason is to preserve the Islamic upbringing of children and maintain the religious harmony of the family. If you have specific circumstances, we recommend speaking with one of our scholars for personalized guidance.

The marriage registration process typically takes 2-4 weeks from the date of application, provided all required documents are submitted correctly. The timeline includes document verification, pre-marital counseling, and the actual marriage contract signing. For couples who have already completed their religious marriage (Nikah) and only need civil registration, the process may be faster, usually within 1-2 weeks.

While Islamic law permits a man to have up to four wives under specific conditions, Ethiopian civil law does not recognize polygamous marriages. The Ethiopian Islamic Affairs Supreme Council follows the national laws of the country, and therefore, we only register monogamous marriages. It's important to note that any additional marriages conducted under religious rites without civil registration are not legally recognized and may have serious legal consequences.

Yes, the Ethiopian Islamic Affairs Supreme Council offers marriage ceremony facilities at our premises. Our main hall can accommodate up to 100 guests and is equipped with necessary audio-visual equipment. The fee for using our premises includes the marriage officiant, basic decoration, and administrative support. Due to high demand, we recommend booking the venue at least one month in advance. Additional services such as photography, catering, and floral arrangements can be arranged through our preferred vendors.
