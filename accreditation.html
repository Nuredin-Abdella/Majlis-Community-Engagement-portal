Official Islamic Accreditation

Get your Islamic institution officially recognized and accredited by the Ethiopian Islamic Affairs Supreme Council.

Our accreditation program ensures that Islamic educational institutions, mosques, and organizations meet the required standards for quality Islamic education and services. Accreditation provides official recognition and helps maintain high standards across all Islamic institutions in Ethiopia.

We evaluate institutions based on curriculum quality, qualified instructors, facilities, governance, and adherence to authentic Islamic teachings.

Accreditation Standards

Key areas we evaluate for accreditation

Curriculum Quality

Authentic Islamic curriculum based on Quran, Sunnah, and recognized Islamic scholarship.

Qualified Staff

Teachers and administrators with proper Islamic education and teaching credentials.

Facilities

Adequate facilities for learning, prayer, and community activities.

Governance

Proper organizational structure and transparent management practices.

Outcomes

Demonstrated positive impact on students and community members.

Compliance

Adherence to national laws and Majlis guidelines.
