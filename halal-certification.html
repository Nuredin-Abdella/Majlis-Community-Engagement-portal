Official Halal Certification

Get your food products, restaurants, and food processing facilities certified as Halal according to Islamic dietary laws.

The Ethiopian Islamic Affairs Supreme Council provides comprehensive halal certification services to ensure that food products and establishments comply with Islamic dietary requirements. Our certification is recognized nationally and helps businesses reach the Muslim consumer market.

Our team of qualified inspectors and Islamic scholars work together to verify compliance with halal standards, from ingredient sourcing to production processes.

Simple steps to get your halal certification

Production process documentation and flow charts

Facility layout and equipment specifications

Supplier certificates for halal ingredients

Cleaning and sanitation procedures
